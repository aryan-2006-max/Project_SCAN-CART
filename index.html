How it works...!

01

Geofencing Intelligence

The app only activates within physical store boundaries, ensuring checkout functionality is location-locked for maximum security and accuracy.

02

Search Product Availability

Instantly search and check if any product is available in the store before you walk the aisles — saving time and ensuring you find what you need.

03

Scan with Mobile Camera

Use your phone's camera to scan product barcodes directly — no extra hardware needed. Just point, scan, and add items to your digital cart instantly.

04

Unique Bill Verification

Post-payment, the app generates a secure, time-limited exit pass QR code that is verified at the door, ensuring only paid items leave the store.
